01 — About

Sound is my
language

I'm Naphat Sudjai, a music producer and audio engineer with a deep passion for building immersive sonic experiences. My journey in audio started out of pure passion — making tracks for fun with friends in a small room — but quickly evolved into a serious career path during my time at university.

Today, I work across multiple dimensions of sound. From beat construction and full-track arrangement to film scoring and complete audio post-production, I bring a meticulous ear and creative instinct to every session. My strongest expertise lies in Mix & Mastering across vocals, instrumentation, and cinematic audio.

My work primarily spans Hip-Hop, Trap, R&B, and Cinematic music — always searching for that specific frequency that makes people feel something. My main tools of choice are Ableton Live and Pro Tools. Beyond audio, I constantly push myself into photography, video editing, music video production, web development, coding, and AI tools.

As a recording artist, I go by Devilheart — a creative alias where I take full ownership of every element: writing, composing, arranging, mixing, mastering, and directing the visuals myself. Every release under Devilheart is entirely self-made, from the first note to the final frame.

Currently seeking an internship at a music & record studio, eager to work alongside industry professionals, contribute to real-world productions, and push my craft to the next level.
