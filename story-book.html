Devilheart's Story Book is a 6-chapter mixtape where each track represents a page in a personal narrative. Written, composed, produced, arranged, mixed and mastered entirely by Naphat Sudjai.

The project is structured like a book — opening with a Prologue and unfolding through five chapters (Page I through Page V), each carrying its own sonic mood and emotional weight.

Every sound, every lyric, every mix decision was made by one person — no outside production, no collaboration. A pure, unfiltered expression of the Devilheart project.
